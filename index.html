Yaowei Hu (胡耀炜)

Ph.D. Candidate

University of Arkansas

Member of the CausalAI Lab

About Me: I am a Ph.D. candidate from the CSCE department at University of Arkansas, advised by Prof. Lu Zhang. Previously, I obtained my M.S. and B.ENG. from the college of computer at Sichuan University in China.

Research Interest: Currently, my research primarily focuses on fair machine learning and causal inference. Specifically, I propose various fairness algorithms to achieve long-term fairness in sequential decision-making. My interests also include generative models, reinforcement learning, natural language processing. Recently, I have been working on downstream applications of LLMs.
